WORLDWIDE WELLINGTON MONTH

October 2023

As Gordon Ramsay's signature dish, hundreds of thousands of Beef Wellingtons are sold across Gordon Ramsay Restaurants globally each year. We're turning this October into Worldwide Wellington month! For the whole month, you can enjoy the classic Beef Wellington across a selection of our UK and international restaurants, from Bread Street Kitchen & Bar in The City of London to Hell's Kitchen in Las Vegas.

To make the month extra special, Bread Street Kitchen, Heddon Street Kitchen, York & Albany, and Gordon Ramsay Bar & Grill will be serving up a special Worldwide Wellington month menu with limited edition wellingtons.

Whether you join us at our restaurants & bars, or prefer to cook at home, read on to discover how all of us at Gordon Ramsay Restaurants are celebrating Worldwide Wellington month this year.

Celebrate in our UK Restaurants

Many of our restaurants are celebrating with brand new menus just for October. Whether you fancy a traditional Beef Wellington or one of our limited edition Venison & Haggis or Triple Chocolate dessert wellingtons, visit us at Bread Street Kitchen, Heddon Street Kitchen, York & Albany, or Gordon Ramsay Bar & Grill.

VIEW THE SPECIAL MENU*

If you're after a luxurious slice of Gordon's signature dish in an opulent setting, join us at Savoy Grill or The River Restaurant in The Savoy Hotel.



*Special menu available at Bread Street Kitchen, Heddon Street Kitchen, York & Albany, and Gordon Ramsay Bar & Grill only.

Wellington Recipes

Create the traditional beef dish in your own home, or try something new with chocolate, vegetables, or lamb. This dish isn't restricted to meat eaters. If you're on a plant based or vegetarian diet, we have developed the Beet Wellington and Nut Roast Wellington that are perfect for you to re-create at home. Looking to hone your cookery skills and learn how to create Gordon's signature dishes? Why not join us on one of our Beef Wellington masterclasses, taught by our expert chefs.

Challenge yourself to make Gordon's Wellington using one of our recipes at home and post your creations on social? Snap and share your pics using the hashtag #WorldwideWellington.
